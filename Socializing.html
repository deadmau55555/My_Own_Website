Socializing:

Socialization is the process through which people are taught to be proficient members of a society. It describes the ways that people come to understand societal norms and expectations, to accept society's beliefs, and to be aware of societal values.
Socialization has three primary goals: teaching impulse control and developing a conscience, preparing people to perform certain social roles, and cultivating shared sources of meaning and value.
So people use social media for example to stay in touch and interact with friends, family and various communities. Businesses use social applications to market and promote their products and track customer concerns. Business-to-consumer websites include social components, such as comment fields for users.
